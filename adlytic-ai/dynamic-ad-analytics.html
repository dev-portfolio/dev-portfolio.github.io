AUDIENCE DEMOGRAPHICS

Get audience demographics of people viewing your ad. Every person in front of the ad (screen) will be classified into the following demographic categories:

Age Groups Gender
  • Child
  • Teen
  • Adult
  • Elderly
  • Male
  • Female

Footfall

Total count of people standing or walking in front of the screen

Gaze Time

Amount of time a person actually looks at the ad

Dwell Time

Average amount of time a person spends in front of the screen

Dynamic Ads

Ads are shown according to the demographics of a person. Targeted ads will be shown to relevant people.
